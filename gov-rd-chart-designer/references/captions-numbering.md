# Captions And Numbering

Figure labels must follow the chapter and the surrounding text. Do not generate isolated or generic figure titles.

## Numbering Rules

- Follow the existing document style first.
- Common style: `图1-1`, `图1-2`, `图2-1`.
- If the document uses multi-level section numbering, adapt to its style, such as `图2.1-1`.
- Appendix figures may use `附图A-1`, `附图B-1`, or the document's existing appendix style.
- Same chapter figures increase by appearance order.
- When editing an existing document, inspect nearby captions and continue their format.

## Caption Rules

Use a caption that connects the chapter topic, project subject, and figure purpose.

For Word/PDF body figures, keep the official caption outside the artwork. Do not place `建议图题`, formal figure numbers, duplicated large captions, or explanatory footer notes inside the image unless the user explicitly asks for a display-style graphic. A short in-artwork theme anchor is allowed when it names the core subject or evidence logic, such as `多智能体中枢（项目核心主体）` or `低空数据资产全生命周期复用`.

Good patterns:

- `图2-1 项目拟解决的关键科学问题与技术瓶颈关系图`
- `图3-1 项目总体技术路线与研发任务耦合关系图`
- `图3-2 多源数据驱动的智能诊断技术原理图`
- `图4-1 关键技术攻关路径及成果转化逻辑图`
- `图5-1 项目考核指标与研发任务对应关系图`

Avoid:

- `总体架构图`
- `流程图`
- `项目图`
- captions that use marketing slogans instead of technical content.
- putting `建议图题：...` inside the image itself.
- duplicating a large in-image caption and a Word caption for the same figure.

## Body Reference Sentences

Provide a sentence that lets the figure enter the document naturally.

Examples:

- `项目拟解决的关键科学问题及其对应技术瓶颈如图2-1所示。`
- `围绕核心技术瓶颈，项目形成“机理建模、算法研发、系统集成、验证应用”递进式技术路线，见图3-1。`
- `项目研发任务与考核指标之间的对应关系如图5-1所示。`

## Explanatory Text After Figure

When useful, provide one short paragraph after the figure that explains the logic, not the visual style.

Keep this paragraph as document text outside the artwork. For matrix figures, do not use bottom notes inside the image to explain what the matrix does; put that explanation before or after the figure in the body text.

Example:

`如图3-1所示，项目以应用场景需求为牵引，首先开展关键机理分析和多源数据治理，在此基础上构建核心模型与算法，并通过系统集成和示范应用完成技术验证，最终支撑项目考核指标实现。`
